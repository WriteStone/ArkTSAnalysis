实际使用的，

 已实现功能的API和按钮组件
定位服务 (Location Services)

- **功能**: 开启/关闭位置服务
- **按钮**: Enable Location Service
- **API**: `geolocation.requestEnableLocation()`
- **权限**: `ohos.permission.MANAGE_SECURE_SETTINGS` (由系统处理，应用无需直接请求)

- **功能**: 获取当前地理位置
- **按钮**: Get Current Location
- **API**: `geolocation.getCurrentLocation(request: CurrentLocationRequest)`
- **权限**: `ohos.permission.LOCATION`, `ohos.permission.APPROXIMATELY_LOCATION`

- **功能**: 获取最后一次可知位置
- **按钮**: Get Last Location
- **API**: `geolocation.getLastLocation()`
- **权限**: `ohos.permission.LOCATION`, `ohos.permission.APPROXIMATELY_LOCATION`

- **功能**: 监听位置变化
- **按钮**: Listen to Location Changes
- **API**: `geolocation.on('locationChange', request: LocationRequest, callback)`
- **权限**: `ohos.permission.LOCATION`, `ohos.permission.APPROXIMATELY_LOCATION`

- **功能**: 获取小区信息
- **按钮**: Get Cell Information
- **API**: `radio.getCellInformation()`
- **权限**: `ohos.permission.LOCATION`, `ohos.permission.APPROXIMATELY_LOCATION`
  **注意**: 此API在API 12中不可用，已做兼容性提示。
